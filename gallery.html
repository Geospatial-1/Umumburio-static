Our Journey Timeline

Key milestones in the growth of Umumburio SHG

2015

Umumburio SHG founded with 25 founding members. First meeting held at Rhino Building.

2017

Registered as official Self Help Group. First loan program launched with KES 500,000 capital.

2019

Reached 100 active members. Launched financial literacy training program.

2021

Survived COVID-19 challenges. Digital record keeping introduced. First community project launched.

2023

Reached 150+ members. Over KES 10 million disbursed in loans. Website launched.
