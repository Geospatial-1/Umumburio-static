Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our loan services

What are the requirements to qualify for a loan?

To qualify for a loan, you must:

  • Be an active member of Umumburio SHG for at least 3 months
  • Have regular savings contributions (at least 70% of required contributions)
  • Attend at least 70% of group meetings
  • Have no outstanding loan balances
  • Provide at least one guarantor for loans above KES 50,000

How long does loan approval take?

Loan approval typically takes 3-7 working days. The process involves:

  • Initial screening by the treasurer (1-2 days)
  • Review by the executive committee (2-3 days)
  • Final approval by the chairperson (1-2 days)

Emergency loans can be processed within 24 hours.

What happens if I miss a payment?

If you miss a payment:

  • A reminder will be sent after 3 days
  • After 7 days, a late fee of 2% will be applied
  • After 30 days, the guarantor will be contacted
  • Persistent default may affect future loan eligibility

We encourage members to communicate any difficulties in advance so we can work out a solution.
